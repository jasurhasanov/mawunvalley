Working Remotely from South Lombok: A Digital Nomad's Guide

By Mawun Valley Team · February 19, 2026

← Back to Field Notes

Written for: Remote workers, digital nomads, and location-independent professionals looking for an alternative to crowded Canggu and overpriced Bali coworking.

If you're burnt out on Bali's traffic, $15 smoothie bowls, and fighting for café tables — South Lombok might be exactly what you need. Fewer nomads means more peace, lower costs, and beaches you can actually have to yourself.

The WiFi Situation (Let's Be Real)

What to Expect

  • Café WiFi: 10-30 Mbps typical in Kuta town
  • Accommodation WiFi: Varies wildly — ask before booking
  • Mobile data (your backup): Telkomsel 4G is decent. Get 20-50GB data (IDR 100-200k)

Pro Tips

  • Always test before booking long stays
  • Have a mobile hotspot as backup
  • Schedule important calls for morning (faster speeds)

Best Places to Work From

Cafés with Good WiFi

  • Milk Espresso: Reliable WiFi, AC, good coffee
  • El Bazar: Nice atmosphere, decent connection
  • Ashtari: Incredible views, WiFi okay

Our Noni's Café at Mawun Valley is a quieter option with farm views — great for focused work.

Monthly Cost Breakdown

Expense Monthly (USD)
Accommodation $260-520
Food $195-325
Scooter $52-78
Other (data, activities) $107-208
Total $615-1,130

Compare that to Canggu where accommodation alone can be $800-1,500/month.

The Nomad Lifestyle Here

A Typical Day

6:30am: Sunrise surf or beach walk
8:00am: Breakfast and emails
9:00am-1:00pm: Deep work
1:00pm: Lunch at a warung (IDR 25-35k)
2:00pm-5:00pm: Lighter tasks or break time
5:30pm: Sunset at one of the viewpoints
7:00pm: Dinner, wind down

Combating Isolation

  • Join group activities (farm experiences, surf lessons, yoga)
  • Become a "regular" at one café
  • Attend community gatherings

Is South Lombok Right for You?

✅ Yes, if you:

  • Can work asynchronously
  • Value peace over nightlife
  • Are self-motivated
  • Want to save money while living well

❌ Maybe not, if you:

  • Need ultra-reliable high-speed internet for constant video calls
  • Want bustling coworking scenes
  • Prefer urban convenience
