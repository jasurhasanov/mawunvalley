Group Travel in South Lombok: The Ultimate Friend Trip Guide

By Mawun Valley Team · February 14, 2026

← Back to Field Notes

Written for: Friend groups in their 20s-30s planning an adventure trip, gap year travellers, birthday groups, and anyone coordinating a squad holiday.

Travelling with friends is one of life's great joys — and South Lombok is practically designed for it. Empty beaches, cheap scooter rentals, sunset sessions, and enough activities to keep everyone happy.

Best Group Accommodation Options

Multiple Cabins or Bungalows

Rather than cramming into one villa, many groups book several cabins at the same property. At Mawun Valley Farm, groups can book our cabins and lodges — you each get your own space but share common areas, meals, and the experience.

Why This Works:

  • Everyone has their own space (crucial for group harmony)
  • Couples can have privacy
  • Common areas for socializing
  • Often cheaper than large villas

Activities That Work for Groups

Everyone Loves: Beach Days

South Lombok's beaches are spacious enough that even large groups don't feel crowded. Check out our beach hopping guide for the best spots.

Selong Belanak is ideal for mixed-ability groups — beginners can surf the gentle whitewash while stronger surfers head to the outside break.

Split & Reconvene Activities

  • Surfers: Dawn patrol at Gerupuk or Selong
  • Adventurous ones: Waterfall chases, cliff jumping
  • Relaxed crew: Beach lounging, massage sessions
  • Curious types: Farm visits, cooking classes

Group-Only Activities

  • Sunset at Bukit Merese: Group photos mandatory.
  • Group surf lesson: Watching your friends fall is half the fun.
  • BBQ night: Our Gather program hosts group dinners.
  • Day trip to Gili Islands: Easy boat trip, snorkeling, beach clubs.

Budget Breakdown for Groups

Typical daily spend per person in a group of 6:

  • Accommodation: IDR 200-350k ($13-23) per person sharing
  • Food: IDR 150-250k ($10-17)
  • Scooter: IDR 35-40k ($2.50) sharing
  • Activities: IDR 100-200k ($7-13)

Total: $33-55 per day per person. Check our budget guide for more tips.

Group Travel Pro Tips

  • Agree on non-negotiables early
  • Create a shared expense tracker — Splitwise saves friendships
  • Schedule free time — even best friends need breaks
  • Book accommodation 2-3 weeks ahead in peak season
