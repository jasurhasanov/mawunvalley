Most people only know the cashew nut. But cashew trees are fascinating plants with a surprising secret: the nut is only a small part of the story.

## What Is a Cashew Tree?

**Anacardium occidentale** is a tropical evergreen tree native to Brazil. Here in Lombok, cashew trees thrive in our warm climate.

- Height: 10-12 meters typical
- Lifespan: 30+ years productive
- Yield: 20-30 kg nuts per tree annually

## The Strange Anatomy

The cashew nut hangs *outside* the fruit, not inside. What we call the "cashew apple" is actually a swollen fruit stem.
