One of my favorite things to make during cashew season.

## Ingredients

- 1 kg ripe cashew apples
- 500g sugar
- Juice of 2 limes
- 1 cup water
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Wash cashew apples, remove seeds, chop roughly
2. Cook with water until soft (about 20 minutes)
3. Blend or mash for texture
4. Add sugar, lime juice, salt
5. Cook until it coats a spoon
6. Pour into sterilized jars
