Forget cookie-cutter resorts. South Lombok offers romance with authenticity.

## Unique Couple Experiences

### Farm Stay Under the Stars
At Mawun Valley Farm, couples can enjoy:
- Private cabin with mountain views
- Candlelit dinners with farm-fresh ingredients
- Sunrise coffee on your balcony

### Sunset Spots

**Bukit Merese** — Panoramic clifftop views  
**Mawun Beach** — Golden hour on quiet sands  
**Selong Belanak** — Watch surfers catch the last waves

## Date Ideas

1. Sunrise motorbike ride to a secret beach
2. Couples massage in Kuta
3. Picnic at the rice fields
4. BBQ night at the farm
5. Stargazing from your cabin deck
