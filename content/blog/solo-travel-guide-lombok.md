Traveling solo in South Lombok is safe, rewarding, and full of opportunities to connect.

## Is South Lombok Safe for Solo Travelers?

**Yes.** The locals are friendly and helpful. Crime rates are low.

## Where to Meet People

- **Farm stays** — Shared meals and activities make meeting people natural
- **Surf spots** — The lineup at Selong Belanak is incredibly social
- **Cafes in Kuta** — Perfect for solo travelers

## Solo Travel Tips

1. Stay longer — the magic happens when you slow down
2. Learn some Indonesian
3. Join activities — cooking classes, farm work, surf lessons
4. Stay connected — get a local SIM
