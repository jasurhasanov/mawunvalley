South Lombok is quickly becoming one of Southeast Asia's premier surf destinations.

## Best Surf Spots

### Selong Belanak
**Level:** Beginner to Intermediate  
Gentle, rolling waves. Perfect for learning or longboarding.

### Mawun Beach
**Level:** Beginner to Intermediate  
Just 5 minutes from the farm. Protected bay with consistent small waves.

### Tampah
**Level:** Intermediate to Advanced  
A left-hand reef break that gets hollow on bigger swells.

### Ekas
**Level:** Intermediate to Advanced  
Multiple breaks in a stunning bay.

## Practical Tips

- **Board rental:** Available at Selong Belanak and Kuta
- **Transport:** Rent a motorbike (50-70K/day)
- **Crowds:** Early morning sessions are quietest
