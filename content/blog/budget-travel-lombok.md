South Lombok offers incredible value.

## Accommodation

### Best Value Options
- **Farm stays with volunteer programs** — Exchange 3 hours of work for accommodation + meals
- **Budget guesthouses in Kuta** — From 150K/night
- **Homestays** — Authentic experience, great prices

## Food & Drink

**Warungs (local eateries):** 15-25K per meal  
**Farm stays with included meals:** Best value  

## Transport

**Motorbike rental:** 50-70K/day  

## Free Activities

- Beach sunsets
- Rice field walks
- Farm visits
- Snorkeling (bring your own gear)

## Daily Budget Estimate

- **Backpacker style:** 300-400K/day
- **Comfortable budget:** 500-700K/day
