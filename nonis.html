Our Story

Less Items, More Quality

Our story at Mawun Valley Farm is about embracing the quiet beauty of slow living. Here, we cultivate peace, open spaces, and a deep connection to nature, nurturing the land's vibrant life.

It's from this same vision that Noni's, our own little resto and pastry, was born. We extend the farm's ethos of simplicity and genuine enjoyment into every dish and delightful treat.

Whether it's a creamy Basque Burnt Cheesecake or a soothing Lemongrass Ginger Tea, everything is crafted with the same dedication we put into nurturing our farm.

As you enjoy your meal, know it's a blend of our slow living aspirations at Mawun Valley Farm and the genuine warmth we bake into every bite at Noni's.
